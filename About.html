Why We Built This

Qatar is growing rapidly as a digital leader. With that growth comes online risks—phishing, fake websites, account takeovers, and data leaks. This website helps you recognise threats early and build safer online habits.

Awareness → Protection

Most attacks succeed because people are rushed, curious, or pressured. Awareness breaks that cycle.

Small Habits, Big Impact

Strong passwords, updates, and 2FA stop a huge percentage of common attacks.

Spot Scams Faster

Learn how scammers create urgency, fake trust, and trick you into clicking or sharing details.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Threats in Qatar: common scams and how they work.
  • Safety tips: quick steps to protect accounts and devices.
  • Interactive pages: security check + quizzes with life scenarios.
  • Resources: trusted links for official guidance.
