Get Tips: Stay Secure Online

Simple habits that protect your accounts, money, and identity in Qatar’s digital world.

Use Strong Passwords

Create a unique password for every account. Avoid names, birthdays, and repeated patterns.

  • 12+ characters
  • Mix letters, numbers, symbols
  • Use a password manager

Turn On Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Even if your password is leaked, 2FA blocks most unauthorised logins.

  • Prefer authenticator apps
  • Keep backup codes safe
  • Enable 2FA on email first

Think Before You Click

Phishing works because of urgency and fear. Pause and verify before acting.

  • Check sender + domain
  • Hover links before clicking
  • Don’t open unknown attachments

Keep Software Updated

Updates fix security holes that attackers actively exploit.

  • Turn on auto-updates
  • Update browser + phone
  • Remove unused apps

Protect Your Privacy

Limit what you share online—scammers use details to target you.

  • Hide phone number on social media
  • Review app permissions
  • Use private accounts where possible

Be Careful With Public Wi-Fi

Public networks can expose logins and personal data.

  • Avoid banking on public Wi-Fi
  • Use mobile data for sensitive tasks
  • Log out after use
